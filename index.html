Why Penguin?

Penguin lets you discover the true penguins up close, while providing many
comforts such as pools, tennis courts, boat rentals and more,
all to help you feel at home, no matter where in the world you are.
Our worldwide goal is to make sure our guests feel great.

Our Antarctica/South Pole locations come with complimentary penguin-watching tours,
and now open, our most exclusive location in the Galápagos Islands!
This location comes with everything you know and love, and more!
Take guided tours to see a rare species of penguin, as well as other wildlife!

When you reserve a suite at one of our locations, 30 percent of your payment(s)
is donated to help research and protect different species of penguin worldwide!
So Penguin Resorts is more than just a resort, it also helps penguins!
Without your support, none of this would ever happen.
